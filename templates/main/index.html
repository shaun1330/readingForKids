'Reading Program for Kids' is for:

  • Parents of children with reading difficulties who want to give their children extra help or an earlier start with learning to read.
  • Children diagnosed with dyslexia who need one-on-one tuition.
  • Students who have English as a second language and need a systematic way to learn the English Alphabetic System.
  • Students who want to improve their comprehension of English texts by revising phonics.
  • This Program engages kids and makes learning to read engaging and stress-free.

The program includes 20 Traditional stories for reading pleasure. These stories are graded and are suitable for the systematic development of reading fluency.

About the Author

Marilyn trained as a teacher-librarian specialising in children's literature and developed a career around her love of books and reading.

She also spent some time as an educational consultant for two publishers visiting many schools and demonstrating educational resources to teachers.

Marilyn has taught English and mathematics to children from the beginning grade to year nine in secondary schools.

She found many opportunities to teach children who had difficulty learning to read. She taught many students in schools and is still does as a private tutor.

After sitting with many frustrated students who did not know how to decode, Marilyn developed a series of lessons that gave them the ability to sound out words quickly.

These lessons are the foundation of "Reading Program for Kids".
